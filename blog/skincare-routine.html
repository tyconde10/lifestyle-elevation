Skin Routine 101: A No-Nonsense Approach

Forget the 12-step routines that leave you confused and broke. Building an effective skincare routine is about focusing on what works and cutting out the noise. Here’s our straightforward guide to achieving healthier skin.

1. Cleanse Wisely

Start and end your day by washing your face with a gentle cleanser. Cleansing removes dirt, oil, and impurities that can cause breakouts. Avoid harsh soaps that strip your skin of its natural oils.

2. Hydrate & Moisturize

Use a hydrating toner or essence followed by a moisturizer suited to your skin type. Hydration keeps your skin barrier strong and reduces dryness or oily patches.

3. Protect with SPF

The single most important step: daily sun protection. Use a broad-spectrum sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher to prevent premature ageing and sun damage.

4. Treat Specific Concerns

If you have specific concerns like acne, dark spots or fine lines, incorporate targeted treatments like a retinol serum or vitamin C. Introduce one product at a time to monitor how your skin reacts.
